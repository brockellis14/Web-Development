Artisan Sourdough, Baked with Passion

Experience the taste of handcrafted sourdough made with organic ingredients, baked to perfection in every loaf.

Why Rustic Sourdough?

At Rustic Sourdough, we craft every loaf with the finest organic ingredients and a passion for tradition. Our bread is naturally leavened, slow-fermented, and packed with nutrients, making it a healthier and tastier choice for your family. Taste the difference of artisanal baking.

Our Signature Sourdough Products

Classic Sourdough

A perfect balance of flavor, texture, and crispiness. Baked with care, this classic sourdough is the heart of our bakery.

Jalapeño & Cheddar Sourdough

A bold, hearty loaf that combines zesty jalapeño heat with the creamy richness of cheddar cheese.

Cinnamon Swirl Sourdough

A sweet and aromatic loaf, perfect for breakfast or dessert, with a delightful cinnamon swirl.
