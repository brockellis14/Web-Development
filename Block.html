The Cataract of Lodore

...The cataract strong

Then plunges along,

Striking and raging

As if a war waging

Its caverns and rocks among;

Rising and leaping,

Sinking and creeping,

Flying and flinging,

Writhing and ringing,

Eddying and whisking,

Spouting and frisking,

Around and around

With endless rebound:

Confounding, astounding,

Dizzying and deafening
